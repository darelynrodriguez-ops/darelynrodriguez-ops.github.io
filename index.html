Rare Flowers

Butterfly Flower

Interests

  • The Rare Butterfly Mimicry is flowers that are renowned for their uncanny resemblance to blue butterflies in flight.
  • Four pale blue petals form the "wings," while a single, darker blue-violet lower petal creates the "body.
  • The prominent stamens and pistil arch outward, completing the illusion of delicate antennae.
